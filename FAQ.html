Frequently Asked Questions

Home / FAQ
How do I book a photography session? +

You can easily book a session through our online booking form on the "Session Information" page. Select the type of photography service you need, pick a date, and confirm the session with a payment.

Can I purchase photos online? +

Yes, our online shop offers a variety of images for purchase. Simply browse through the "Galleries" section, add your desired photos to the cart, and complete your purchase using our secure payment gateway.

Do you offer customer support? +

Yes, we provide live chat support directly on our website. You can access it through the "Contact" page or by clicking the chat icon located in the bottom right corner of any page.

Can I crop or edit photos before purchasing? +

Yes, our website offers a built-in cropping tool. Once you choose an image from our gallery, you can adjust the dimensions and framing to fit your needs before completing your purchase.

Where can I find your pricing information? +

We provide detailed pricing information for all our services on the "Pricing" page. You can view package options for weddings, portraits, corporate events, and more.

What is the turnaround time for receiving edited photos? +

Our typical turnaround time for delivering edited photos is 7 to 14 business days, depending on the scope of the project and the number of photos being edited.

Can I request specific edits or retouching for my photos? +

Yes, we offer custom editing services! When you book your session or purchase photos, you can provide detailed instructions on the edits or retouching you'd like, and we'll do our best to accommodate your needs.
